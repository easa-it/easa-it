Our Department

Welcome to the Department of IT@EASA CET

EASA College of Engineering & Technology, established in 2001, is a premier institution located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Spread over 50 acres of lush green campus, we provide an ideal environment for academic excellence and holistic development.

Our Department of Information Technology is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum designed to meet the evolving needs of the IT industry.

The campus features modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, a central library with digital resources, sports facilities, and student hostels to provide a comprehensive educational experience.

Our Vision & Mission

Department Vision

To be a world-class hub of excellence in Information Technology, fostering higher-level learning, cutting-edge research, and innovative technologies that transform students into global IT leaders and innovators.

Department Mission

DELIVER high-quality IT education that aligns with student aspirations and potential, equipping them with cutting-edge skills for a dynamic digital world.

INSPIRE a passion for learning and innovation by fostering creativity, problem-solving, and hands-on experience with emerging technologies.

TRANSFORM talents into socially responsible IT professionals who leverage technology to address real-world challenges and serve society ethically.

Program Educational Objectives & Outcomes

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

What our graduates are expected to achieve within 3-5 years after graduation:

  • PEO 1: Graduates will have a sound foundation in the mathematical, scientific and engineering fundamentals to formulate, solve, and analyze problems related to Information Technology.
  • PEO 2: Graduates will have employment in IT industries, and will be socially responsible and integrated with professional and ethical skills.
  • PEO 3: Graduates will be involved in research, higher studies and/or will become entrepreneurs in the long run.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

What our graduates should be able to do at the time of graduation:

  • PSO 1: Have proficiency in programming skills to design, develop and apply appropriate techniques to solve complex engineering problems.
  • PSO 2: Have knowledge to build, automate and manage IT solutions using cutting-edge technologies.
  • PSO 3: Have the ability to research, create, and build new technology solutions that solve real-world problems and move the industry forward.
